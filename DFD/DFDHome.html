Dynamic Force (DF) is a performance dance program designed to unite and prepare youth of every race, religion and socio-economic background to partake in dance training at its best. The program is focused with the techniques of Jazz and Hip Hop, specialized with the elements of lyrical ballet. We strive to make our dancers realize their full potential as active dancers of their communities. Our curriculum is built on confidence, coordination and social skills. Dynamic Force is an instructional and competitive dance program, for boys and girls (grades 3rd-8th grade) designed to help your child learn fundamental skills, while performing or competing in a fun and supportive environment. We are looking for kids who enjoy the art.
